Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16/06/2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Ai Agent And Model Development ("we", "our", "the app") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our application, including its integration with the LinkedIn API.

1. Information We Collect

When you connect your LinkedIn account or Company Page to this application, we may collect:

  • LinkedIn OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens
  • Your LinkedIn organization URN and basic Company Page information
  • Content you submit for publishing (post text, images, links)
  • Basic profile information required to authenticate API requests

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely to:

  • Authenticate requests to the LinkedIn API
  • Publish posts to the connected LinkedIn Company Page on your behalf
  • Maintain and troubleshoot the functionality of the application

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the core functionality described above.

3. Data Storage and Security

Access tokens and related credentials are stored securely and are used only for making authorized API calls to LinkedIn. We apply reasonable technical safeguards to protect this data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties, except where required to operate the core functionality (e.g., direct communication with LinkedIn's API) or where required by law.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the service. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at the email address below. Upon request, we will delete all stored tokens and associated data within a reasonable timeframe.

6. Your Rights

You may revoke this application's access to your LinkedIn account or Company Page at any time via your LinkedIn account settings. You may also request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold about you.

7. LinkedIn API Compliance

This application uses the LinkedIn API and is bound by the LinkedIn API Terms of Use and LinkedIn's Developer Policies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
